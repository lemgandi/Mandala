Make Playdate Video

5 Nov 2024


1. Start playdate simulator:

    /home/devel/PlaydateSDK/bin/PlaydateSimulator Mandala.pdx
2. Start SimpleScreenRecorder from icon or:
   simplescreenrecorder

3. Choose rectangle on simulator.

4. Choose filename, Start Recording, make simulator go

5. Pause Recording

6. convert
   convert_to_gif fn.mkv

7. Check gif in browser
